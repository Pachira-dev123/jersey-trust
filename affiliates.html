FAQ

What counts as a completed setup?
Entity established + bank account opened (or foundation established) + initial invoice paid. If a client chooses a different SKU, we attribute to adjusted fees.
Do I get paid on maintenance?
Yes: 10% of Year‑1 maintenance and 5% in Years 2–3 under Rev‑Share/Tiered models.
How do I track my referrals?
You'll receive a dashboard (or monthly statement) with deal stage, fees collected, and pending payouts. All links use signed IDs and 90‑day cookies.
Can I use paid ads?
Yes, except on Jersey Trust brand terms or prohibited claims. Share your creatives for quick compliance review.
