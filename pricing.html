FAQs

Are bank accounts guaranteed?
Banking is subject to eligibility and compliance approval. Our edge is preparation: we package KYB/KYC for crypto‑linked revenue so partner banks can approve faster and with fewer queries.
How long does residency (Emirates ID) take?
Typical timelines: company in ~5–10 business days, visas in ~2–3 weeks after establishment (varies by free zone/mainland and nationality).
What currencies can I off‑ramp to?
AED, USD, EUR are commonly supported. We'll confirm options during onboarding with your chosen banking partners.
Can you work with existing companies?
Yes. We can optimise structure, open additional bank accounts, and take over compliance & renewals.
